⚽ How it works

  1. The consolation-bracket teams are in the lottery for picks 2–6. The better the consolation finish, the better the odds at the top pick — see the pot below.
  2. It's a 1–100 draw: each team owns a slice of numbers sized to its odds. We draw a number, whoever's slice it lands in takes the next pick, then draw again (skipping anyone already in) until the order is set.
  3. The whole draw runs off one agreed-upon starting number, so it plays out the same for everyone and can be replayed. Want to sanity-check the odds? Hit Verify the odds at the bottom.
